01

Design vision

Most privacy tools either frighten people or overwhelm them. LeakShield AI takes the opposite stance: it behaves like a calm, executive command center — the kind of dark, considered interface you'd trust to run something important. Risk is communicated with precision, never alarm.

The product is AI-native from the core. The copilot doesn't sit in a corner waiting to chat; it reads your exposure, ranks what matters, drafts the work, and sequences a plan — while the human stays the decision-maker on every action.

Principle
Clarity over alarm

Restrained risk colors, no red walls.

Principle
Decision-ready

Every finding ends in an action.

Principle
AI you steer

Drafts & recommends; you approve.

Principle
Privacy-first

Consent-led, no stored passwords.

02

UX architecture

Information is organized along the user's mental model: Monitor → Find → Act. You set up what's watched, review what was found, then work a prioritized plan. The copilot spans all three as a persistent context-aware layer.

Monitor

Dashboard & Identity Intake — what we watch and your current standing.

Find

Breach Intelligence, Public Footprint, Data Brokers — classified findings.

Act

AI Copilot & Task Board — remediation, generation, tracking.

Primary user flow

Landing→ Consent→ Identity Intake→ Scan→ Dashboard + Score→ Copilot plan→ Remediate & track
03

Navigation

A persistent left rail grouped by Monitor / Findings / Act / Settings, with live counts as ambient signal. The center column is the workspace. A persistent copilot rail docks on the right on desktop and becomes a slide-over on smaller screens — always one tap away, never in the way.

  • Left rail — sectioned destinations, badge counts (4 breaches, 6 findings, 9 tasks), user identity footer.
  • Top bar — contextual title, global search, copilot toggle, alerts.
  • Copilot rail — context summary, next best action, and the live plan, everywhere you go.

08

Executive dashboard

The dashboard answers three questions at a glance: How exposed am I? What's the single most important thing to do? Am I making progress?

  • Privacy Exposure Score — a 0–100 composite (breach severity, credential reuse, footprint, brokers) with a 30-day trend and a plain-language "why."
  • Risk tiles — Breach, Footprint, Old Accounts, Data Brokers — each a one-tap entry into its module.
  • AI recommended next action — the highest impact ÷ effort fix, with projected score gain.
  • Remediation progress + high-risk exposed data + top breaches.

Three layouts ship as live options in the prototype: Executive (hero score + AI action), Grid (uniform metrics), and Focus (single priority feed). The score itself renders as numeric, ring, or banded meter.

09

AI Remediation Copilot

The copilot is the product's spine. It appears as a persistent context-aware rail that reframes itself per screen, plus inline AI cards inside every module. It is useful, ethical, and premium by design.

✦ Useful

Sequences a Today / This Week / Later plan by impact ÷ effort; drafts deletion requests and alias strategy.

✦ Ethical

Every output is labeled, reviewable, and never sent automatically. Confidence is shown, not hidden.

✦ Premium

Live "thinking" summaries, clean confidence meters, and a score projection that makes effort feel worth it.

Surface modes ship as a live toggle: a persistent rail (default) or inline AI cards woven through each module for a lighter footprint.

✦ Copilot · example

"You have 2 critical items tied to one reused password. Fixing that single credential closes your largest gap — about +12 points. I've drafted the broker deletion request; review and send when ready."

10

MVP roadmap — next phase

The prototype is the visual + UX source of truth. The recommended build path, privacy-first throughout:

Phase 1
Foundation
Ship the shell.

React + Vite + TypeScript + Tailwind. Tokenize this design system, build the component kit, wire navigation and simulated data. Dark-mode-first, responsive.

Phase 2
Real signal
Connect detection.

Serverless functions (keys server-side) for breach lookup, footprint & broker scans. One-way credential matching — never store passwords. Per-source confidence.

Phase 3
AI core
Ground the copilot.

Server-side LLM orchestration for scoring rationale, prioritization, and request drafting. Human-in-the-loop gates on every action; full audit trail.

Phase 4
Closing the loop
Automate remediation.

Tracked deletion pipelines, recurring monitoring & alerts, alias provisioning. Honest status — no guarantees of complete removal.

Guardrails carried into every phase

Own/authorized data only · no stored passwords · no real passwords requested · keys stay server-side · no simulated hacking · no third-party lookups without consent · clear disclaimers · no absolute legal promises.
